Frequently Asked Questions

What is the 6·1·2 Method?

The 6·1·2 Method is My Numerology Blueprint's proprietary framework that reads your Life Path, Birth Day, and Birth Month as an interconnected system — not in isolation. These three numbers are then interpreted in the context of your current Personal Year cycle, making your reading timely rather than static.

What is the Northstar?

The Northstar is the AI guide inside My Numerology Blueprint. It knows your full 6·1·2 chart and can answer questions about your numbers, their interplay, and what they mean for where you are right now. Free users get 3 Northstar conversations. Luminary subscribers get unlimited access.

How do I restore my subscription?

Open the Northstar screen and tap the UPGRADE button, then tap Restore purchases at the bottom of the paywall screen. Make sure you are signed in to the same Apple ID you used to purchase.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to your iPhone Settings → tap your name → Subscriptions → My Numerology Blueprint → Cancel Subscription. You will retain access until the end of the current billing period.

My daily notification is not arriving — what should I check?

  • Go to Settings in the app and confirm notifications are enabled and the time is set correctly
  • Go to iPhone Settings → My Numerology Blueprint → Notifications → confirm Allow Notifications is on
  • Make sure your iPhone is not in Focus or Do Not Disturb mode at the scheduled time

Is my data private?

Yes. Your birth date and name are stored locally on your device only and are never uploaded to our servers. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

How do I reset the app?

Delete the app from your iPhone and reinstall it from the App Store. This will clear all locally stored data and restart the onboarding flow. Note that your subscription is tied to your Apple ID, not the app — it can be restored after reinstalling.
