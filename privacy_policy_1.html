Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 June 2026

My Numerology Blueprint is built on a simple principle: your personal data stays on your device. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app uses, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

Information We Collect

Information you provide

  • Birth date — used to calculate your numerology chart (Life Path, Birth Day, Birth Month, Personal Year, Personal Month, Personal Day numbers). Stored locally on your device only.
  • Full birth name (optional) — used to calculate your Expression and Soul Urge numbers if provided. Stored locally on your device only.
  • Notification time preference — stored locally on your device only.

Information collected automatically

  • Subscription status — managed by RevenueCat. RevenueCat collects a random anonymous user identifier to manage your subscription. No personally identifiable information is shared.
  • Advertising data — the free version of the app displays ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. Premium subscribers do not see ads.

How We Use Your Information

  • To generate your numerology chart and daily readings within the app
  • To display relevant advertisements to free users via Google AdMob
  • To process subscription purchases via RevenueCat and Apple In-App Purchase
  • To send your daily Personal Day notification at your chosen time

Third-Party Services

My Numerology Blueprint uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Anthropic — powers the Northstar AI guide. Your chart numbers and questions are sent to Anthropic's Claude API. No personally identifiable information is sent. Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob — displays advertisements to free users and may collect advertising identifiers. Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat — manages subscription purchases and status. Privacy Policy

Data Storage

All personal data (birth date, name, notification preferences) is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's standard local storage. Deleting the app removes all locally stored data. We do not operate our own database of user data.

The Northstar (AI Feature)

The Northstar feature sends your numerology chart numbers — not your birth date or name — and your typed questions to Anthropic's Claude API to generate responses. No personally identifiable information is sent to Anthropic.

Push Notifications

Daily notifications are generated entirely on your device using your locally stored birth date. No data is sent to external servers to generate notifications.

Children's Privacy

My Numerology Blueprint is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Rights

You may delete all app data at any time by deleting the app from your device. Since we do not store personal data on our servers, there is no data for us to delete or provide.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
